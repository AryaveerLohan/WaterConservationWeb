The Issue

Fresh water is limited, but people often waste it. Rivers, lakes, and groundwater sources are getting depleted due to excessive use and pollution.

Causes

  • Leaving taps running unnecessarily.
  • Water pollution from industries and households.
  • Overuse of groundwater.
  • Deforestation reducing groundwater recharge.
  • Leakage in water supply systems.

Effects

  • Shortage of drinking water.
  • Loss of aquatic life.
  • Droughts and water scarcity.
  • Reduced agricultural production.
  • Health problems due to contaminated water.
